# PSE bank codes

Use one of these values in `pse.bank` for PSE payins (`POST /payins`,
`rail: "PSE"`) and PSE payment links (`POST /payment-links`, `rail: "PSE"`).

`pse.personType` (payins only) ∈ `INDIVIDUAL` (personal account) or `BUSINESS`
(company / legal entity).
